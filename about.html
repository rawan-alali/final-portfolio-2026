About Me

Rawan Al Ali

I’m a Business and Interactive Media student interested in storytelling, design, and the way people experience digital spaces. I like creating work that sits between structure and expression, where visuals, sound, motion, and interaction come together to make ideas feel more alive.

This portfolio brings together my Communications Lab projects through the theme of expression. Each project explores a different way of communicating, from observation and listening to memory, interaction, and narrative.

Thinking

The starting point of each project, where ideas become structure, pacing, and interaction.

Seeing

Stories built through observation, detail, perspective, and the act of questioning what is visible.

Listening

Atmospheres shaped through sound, dialogue, rhythm, and the emotional spaces between moments.

Storytelling

Narratives that unfold through interaction, memory, belonging, and the choices users make.
